Welcome to Misty Moon Broadcasting!

Let your musical tastes run free with the Misty Moon stream, which pays no mind to genre, era or place of origin. Give them a nice big stretch with a collection that takes ‘music’ to include poetry, speech and sounds. It’s a myriad mixture for sure, but then everything seems a little queer at first by the light of a misty moon...

About

Misty Moon Broadcasting is a not-for-profit internet radio station. Our idea is that people who love and appreciate music deserve access to the best of everything. So our aim is to give listeners the opportunity to explore beyond their usual confines of genre, era, and place in the world. We curate the database, adding new content is added each month, on the day of the new moon. But the rest is up to chance as the stream shuffles randomly. Expect to hear:

  • -- tracks from folk, jazz, classical and popular traditions;
  • -- poetry, sound art, soundscapes and speech;
  • -- contemporary sounds and those from decades and centuries past;
  • -- womxn, poc and queers;
  • -- tracks range between 1 minute and half an hour.

Expect to travel between:

  • -- east, south-east, southern and central asias;
  • -- north and south americas;
  • -- central america and the caribbean;
  • -- sub-saharan africa;
  • -- the middle east and north africa;
  • -- europe;
  • -- the pacific.

Our musical tastes are growing and are being challenged through this project; we hope by listening to it yours will be too. The stream is managed and funded entirely by volunteers from the MMBC arts collective, based in Brighton, UK. The stream is licenced by PPL and PRS Music, with other licences kindly donated by rights holders of particular compositions and recordings on an ad hoc basis. Eventually we intend to add original content too. Requests, recommendations, submission, comments and correspondence all encouraged: mistymoonbroadcasting@gmail.com
